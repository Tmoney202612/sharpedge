💡 How this works — in plain English

EV % is your edge — how much value you get vs. the true probability. A +9% EV bet at +360 means the math says you should profit long-term, not that you'll win this specific bet.

Many picks are long shots (+300 or higher) because that's where +EV usually lives. Books make their money on heavy favorites by charging extra vig — dogs have wider fair-line gaps, so real edges show up there more often.

Expected hit rate: A +400 pick with 22% true probability should win about 22 of every 100 times. You lose 78. But 22 wins × $400 profit = $8,800 vs 78 losses × $100 = $7,800. Net +$1,000 long term.

PENDING badge: The percentage shown is the implied probability of the bet hitting. It updates to WIN or LOSS once the game ends.

💡 How this works — in plain English

Pinnacle is a sportsbook that never limits sharp bettors. Their no-vig fair line reveals the TRUE probability of each outcome. When a US book offers better odds than that fair line, you have +EV.

EDGE % is how much value you're getting vs Pinnacle's fair line. The "was" number under each book shows where the line was 6 hours ago — watch for books moving against each other (sharp action).

What to do: Find a card with high EDGE %. Bet that team at the book listed in the play box. Strong edges (2%+) are rare — take them when they appear.

💰 Strong Edge = 2%+ above Pinnacle fair line ↩ Sharp Edge = 1%+ above Pinnacle fair line
