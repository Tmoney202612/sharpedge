🔥 Sharp Money Tracker
Where sharp money is going — Pinnacle vs public books, line gaps, steam moves.
💡 How this works — in plain English

Pinnacle is a sportsbook that never limits sharp bettors. That means when their odds look different from DraftKings or FanDuel, it's because professional bettors have already bet that line.

The LINE GAP tells you how different Pinnacle's odds are from the public books. A big gap = strong signal that sharp money is on one side.

What to do: Find a game with a big gap. Check which team Pinnacle has at a much lower number (closer to -100). That's the side sharp money is on. Bet it at DraftKings or FanDuel where the odds are better.

💰 Heavy Sharp = 25+ point gap between books ↩ Reverse Line = sharp money going against the public
